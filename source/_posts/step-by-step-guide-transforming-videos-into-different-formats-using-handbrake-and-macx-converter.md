Please note the latest Handbrake version is compatible with Mac OS X El Capitan, so if you've upgraded to 10.12 macOS Sierra, please head over to a Handbrake alternative for Mac. 

**Step 1: Load Source Video**

After downloading Handbrake, click "Source" to add video such as **MP4, FLV, 3GP, AVI, MKV, MOV, MOD, WMV**, etc. Though supporting any video, Handbrake can convert video on Mac one by one only, so if you are longing for transcoding more than one video, please seek for an alternative to Handbrake Mac that can decode several video files at one stroke.
