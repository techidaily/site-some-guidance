## How to Flip/Rotate Sideway Video in Handbrake

_Case 1: I'd like to use [Handbrake to convert video](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) & compress some video that was taken via a camera that was mounted upside down. Can HandBrake flip/rotate a video?_ 

**Solution:** Videos may look good on the camera but go something wrong when it is put on the computer. But Version 1.0.0 makes it possible to rotate MP4 videos and other files, though it's a bit of complicated thing. Here is the detailed guide about how to use Handbrake to rotate video clockwise/counterclockwise by 90, 180, and 270 degrees to avoid upside down video.   
 Related: [Handbrake rip DVD tutorial](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/), [Handbrake for Mac](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/), [Handbrake convert mkv](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/)

Step 1: Free [download and install Handbrake](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) and open it.  
 Step 2: Select the input video by tapping "Source".  
 Step 3: Go to "Browse" -> "Destination" to save the output video.   
 Step 4: Go to "Video" -> "Extra Options" Window and input the keywords as below. 

Detailed commands to stop Handbrake from turning video upside down

![Handbrake rotate video](https://www.macxdvd.com/mac-dvd-video-converter-how-to/article-image/handbrake-rotate-1.jpg) 

Handbrake rotate video

1. If you want an x flip in Handbrake, please enter the command ", --rotate=1" into the "Extra Options" Window.
2. If you want a y flip in Handbrake, please enter the command ", --rotate=2" into the "Extra Options" Window.
3. To turn video upside down or [rotate MP4 video](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) 180 degrees, put the command ", --rotate=3" into the "Extra Options" Window. This is also the default setting in Handbrake rotating video documentation.
4. If you want to rotate a video in 90 degrees clockwise, put the command ", --rotate=4" into the "Extra Options" Window.
5. To do a 90 degrees rotate plus y flip, put the command ", --rotate=5" into the "Extra Options" Window.
6. To do a 270 degrees rotate plus y flip, put the command ", --rotate=6" into the "Extra Options" Window.
7. To rotate video 90 degree anti-clockwise or process 270 degrees rotate in Handbrake, put the command ", --rotate=7" into the "Extra Options" Window.
