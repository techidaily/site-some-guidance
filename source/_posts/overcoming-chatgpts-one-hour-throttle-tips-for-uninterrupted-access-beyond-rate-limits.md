##  Why ChatGPT Shows a "Too Many Requests in an Hour" Error

 ChatGPT displays a "Too many requests in an hour. Try again later" message when you've entered too many queries in a short period of time. This error locks you out of the site and prevents you from entering further queries.

 While ChatGPT has no official guidelines as to [how many questions you can ask in an hour](https://youtube-video-recordings.techidaily.com/pathway-to-profit-via-youtubes-cp2024-program/), prompting the tool with too many queries in a short span of time can trigger the tool to lock you out.

###  Refresh Your ChatGPT Web Page

 One reason you get the above error is that your web browser has a minor glitch. In this case, [reload your current web page](https://some-approaches.techidaily.com/2024-approved-turning-images-into-scenes-syncing-beats/) to possibly fix your issue.

 To do that, beside the address bar in your web browser, click the refresh icon.

![Select the refresh icon.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/1-refresh-chatgpt-web-page.png) 

 When your web page has reloaded, see if you can enter your queries.

###  Check If ChatGPT Is Down

 Another reason you get a "too many requests an hour" error is that [ChatGPT is down](https://remote-screen-capture.techidaily.com/2024-approved-game-changing-ps4-screen-recordings-using-obs/). The site's servers may be experiencing issues, being unable to serve your queries.

 In this case, head to the [OpenAI Status](https://status.openai.com/) site and check the ChatGPT's current status. If this site tells you that the platform is down, wait until the issues are fixed and [the site is back up](https://extra-approaches.techidaily.com/new-price-tag-shooting-your-next-music-video/).

 ChatGPT's "Too many requests in an hour. Try again later" message is tied to [your account on the site](https://youtube-videos.techidaily.com/channel-catalysts-affiliates-aiding-budgeted-buzzers-for-2024/). This means, if you switch to another account, you can possibly fix your issue.

 To do that, either create a new ChatGPT account or use the secondary account you already have. That will help you get around the above error on the site.
