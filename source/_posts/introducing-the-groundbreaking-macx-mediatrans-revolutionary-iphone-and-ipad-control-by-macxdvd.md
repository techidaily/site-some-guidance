## Introducing the Groundbreaking MacX MediaTrans: Revolutionary iPhone and iPad Control by MacXDVD

## MacXDVD Announces Debut of MacX MediaTrans - a Crowning Achievement in iPhone iPad Management 

Jul 12, 2016

![MacX MediaTrans Release](https://www.macxdvd.com/press-room/image/media.png) 

_MacXDVD today announces the official release of MacX MediaTrans - an epoch-making achievement in the field of iPad iPhone content management. It introduces a set of breakthrough features that are meticulously tailored to manage music, backup photos and transfer videos between iPhone iPad and Mac in one finger tap._ 

Photos, videos and music are usually to blame for iPhone storage woes, and managing these media files has always remained challenging for many. In a bid to put an end to all that crisis, MacXDVD has finally come with a new course of iOS content management and announces the official release of MacX MediaTrans. Now the revolutionary iPad iPhone manager for Mac perfectly integrates with up-to-the-minute photo backup, music manager, video transfer and USB drive in demand, thus free up iDevice's space and get media files well under control. 

For more detailed info of the iPhone transfer software, please visit: [http://www.macxdvd.com/mediatrans/](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) 

"iPhone iPad users still cringe in horror of data erasing when having to sync media file with their computers, just in case of a hard drive crash. On the other hand, they blame for no way of exporting their files to Macs as a backup with iTunes, especially when they need to switch to a new phone, free up their storage space or set about an iOS upgrade." said Jack, CEO of MacXDVD Software. "But we are proud to say that these three can be fixed with MacX MediaTrans, which can backup and manage iPhone iPad iPod users' resources, without erasing, dumping or cluttering their media files." 

The release of [**MacX MediaTrans**](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) gives users more efficiency and convenience in iPhone iPad management: transfer every piece of iOS media content, from a whole library of music to any photo or 4k video. Check all highlight features of MacX MediaTrans below: 

**Manage music** \- Create, delete and edit playlist at will; go way beyond what iTunes offer - export music from iPhone iPod to Mac, and reversely, with tracks, artists and genres all under control. 

**Transfer video back and forth** – Support two-way transfer between iDevices and Mac; free to put all 4k videos, movies, or MKV HD recordings, right into iPhone 6S or iPad Pro with no format issue; auto rotate sideways video to make it right away up. 

**Backup photo** – Transfer pictures, live photos or even entire library from iPhone iPad to Mac (macOS Sierra) in batch in bullet-fast speed.

**Use iPhone as a USB Flash** – Use iPhone as a USB flash drive in a way more secure to clone all types of items, from Word to PDF, Excel, DMG, EXE, Apps, etc. 

On the subjects of speed and easiness during file transfer, Jack added:" The iPhone manager jumps the step and cuts down the transferring time by guiding Mac users to transfer file with drag-n-drop, making it easier than ever to backup, manage, import & export music, videos and videos, all in one location." Internal test shows that it takes only 8 seconds to backup the huge 100 4k photos. 

**System Requirement:** 

According to its tech spec, MacX MediaTrans is effective on even the latest models of the Apple iPhone iPad and iPod, including iPhone 6S and iPad Pro, and works perfectly with the newest macOS Sierra. Check the detailed system requirement below: 

1\. Operating OS:  
OS: Mac OS X 10.7 or above (including macOS Sierra)  
iOS: iOS 7 or above (including the latest iOS 9)  
2\. iTunes: iTunes 11.2 or above. 

**Price and Availability**

MacX MediaTrans is set to be accessible at 29.95 USD for 1 Mac with 1 year free upgrade privilege. What's more, the iPhone iPad manager for Mac comes with a lifetime-licensed edition and a family-licensed version, which charge 35.95 USD with 2 Macs usability and 65.95 USD applicable to 6 Macs, respectively, yet without function or service time limitation.
