### How to Use VLC Mac to Extract Audio from DVD Disc

Step 1: Launch VLC media player > click Media on the top menu of the home screen.

Step 2: Choose "Convert/save..." > select "Disc" on Open Media window.

Step 3: Tap "Browse" to load your input DVD disc > move your mouse to the lower right-corner of the window to click "Convert /Save" button.
