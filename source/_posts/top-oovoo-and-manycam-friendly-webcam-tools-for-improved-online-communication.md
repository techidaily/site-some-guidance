## Top OoVoo & ManyCam-Friendly Webcam Tools for Improved Online Communication

## ManyCam for ooVoo

Search 

In ooVoo, open "Settings" (Main menu - File - Settings...) and then click on the "Audio and Video" menu item. Click on "Check your audio & video devices - Check your Webcam- Change webcam". Select "ManyCam Virtual Webcam" or "ManyCam Video Source".

![](https://manycam.com/build/images/help/settings/oovoo.jpg?v=005f3e0707)   
  

  
To choose ManyCam as the audio source, open "Settings" (Main menu - File - Settings...) and then click on the "Audio and Video" menu item. Click on "Check your audio & video devices - Check your Microphone - Change microphone". Select "ManyCam Virtual Microphone".

![](https://manycam.com/build/images/help/settings/audio/oovoo.jpg?v=60c3695653)
