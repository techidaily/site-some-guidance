**Cons**: brim with confusing download buttons, ads and virus, high privacy and copyright risk. 

### 2\. MP3.com

**Pros**: a wide collection of genres and songs from different time period, free download music for iPad, no register required, bring many songs from unknown artists and bands.

**Cons**: limited hit singles, flashing ads, not easy to search songs, few a-list pop artist. 

### 3\. Jamendo

**Pros**: boast over 473,000 tracks and 40,000 artists for songs download on iPad, a great site to dig out great new artists, songs are well-chunked, most download, most played, latest release, access to upload your music.

**Cons**: users can not download all music for iPad for free, music delivery includes Bit Torrent.

### 4\. [Gaana.com](http://gaana.com/)

**Pros**: tons and tons of free Bollywood songs, totally free, nifty web design, many radio channel, users can install its app to download music on iPad. 

**Cons**: register required, only Bollywood songs available, have English songs but sung by Bollywood star.

### 5\. BeeMP3

**Pros**: download any free music on iPad with no limits, artist list from A to Z, 1to 9, clean interface.

**Cons**: users may download plugins unconsciously, not one-click download, too many unrelated results, strict user terms and conditions.

### 6\. Mp3tunes.org

**Pros**: different songs charts, boast both English and Hindi free music download to iPad, users can share favorite songs to Facebook, Twitter, social networking sites. 

**Cons**: ads and plugins, confusing download buttons, slow download speed, offer less free songs storage.

### 7\. NoiseTrade

**Pros**: individual free music downloads for iPad, embrace exclusive samplers and releases, easy to share your favorite songs with your friend.

**Cons**: need to provide your email address and postal code in turn, pay what you want to download or tell 3 friends about an artist to download free music to iPad.

### 8\. iHeart

**Pros**: one of the [best free music downloads site](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) for iPad in US, a wide range of genres and radio, users can rate songs, albums.

**Cons**: users can not fast forward, record and rate the new tracks on like stations. Register required. 

### 9\. Xbox Music

**Pros**: millions of songs to free download to iPad, Xbox Music app is available now to download free music on iPad, music library is better than Spotify or Pandora, clean interface.

**Cons**: only 30 days free trial, no different songs genres list, no lyrics.

### 10\. Soundowl

**Pros**: provides free music downloads for iPad, features songs in kinds of genre, rock, pop, country, metal, blues, dance, and more, users can also login in and upload songs, few ads and plugins. 

**Cons**: sometimes slow in responding, register required if users want to upload songs.
